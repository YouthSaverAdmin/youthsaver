Healthy Food Recommendations

Healthy Salad

Ingredients: Mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, avocados, olive oil, lemon juice.

Instructions

  • 1. Wash and chop the mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and avocados.
  • 2. Combine all the chopped ingredients in a bowl.
  • 3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice to taste.
  • 4. Toss gently to mix the salad evenly.
  • 5. Enjoy a fresh, nutritious, and refreshing meal!

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients: Bananas, berries, spinach, almond milk.

Instructions

  • 1. Blend the bananas, berries, and spinach together until smooth.
  • 2. Add the almond milk to the blender and blend again until well combined.
  • 3. This smoothie offers a quick, nutritious option with antioxidants and essential nutrients.

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken breast, olive oil, garlic, herbs.

Instructions

  • 1. Coat chicken breast with olive oil, minced garlic, and your favorite herbs. Marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  • 2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken breast for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  • 3. Slice and serve the grilled chicken breast with your choice of sides.

Creating nutritious, balanced meals, such as grilled chicken with veggies, a spinach and avocado salad, and a smoothie, can positively impact a Filipino college student's mental health. These dishes offer essential nutrients and antioxidants that can help improve mood and cognitive function. Preparing healthy meals encourages self-care and can reduce stress levels. The act of cooking and enjoying nutritious food can serve as a soothing, mindful practice. By incorporating balanced meals into their routine, Filipino students can better manage academic and personal pressures.

Supporting Your Mental Health in College

College life in the Philippines can be both exciting and challenging. With academic pressures, adjusting to new environments, and balancing personal responsibilities, many students may experience stress, anxiety, or burnout at times. Recognizing when you need help and seeking support is essential for maintaining your well-being.
